My Love Story

The Bride's Love Story

#KAYLB 2022

I had always thought about relationships as being sacred, so while growing up there were principles I kept in place for myself as a means of checkmating my activities, of which one is dating someone who has value for me and loves God.


I met Kay in 2013 as a first-year student trying to run her registration, as a final-year student then and also a representative of the JCCF he was there to also help other 100 Level students make the necessary payment with ease, my sister who graduated from the same university knew him and made the introduction because she was with me at the time... He was very helpful, he brought me to ESM where I stayed, served and grew. Fast Forward to two years later when we clicked... I didn't know from the onset that we were going to get this far, because we had a lot of things that were hindrances to us having a smooth sail, but we were both committed to making it work and trust me when I say having Jesus in your life/ relationship is the best thing that can happen to anyone, Jesus kept us, Jesus renews our Love, Jesus is bringing us together as Man and Wife come 17th Dec. 2022 February 2022 made it 7 years of being here, I thank God that in those years, he helped us not lose sight of his presence.

If Love, Gentleness, sacrifice, caring, patience, contentment, and self-control have a name it will be Kalu Chizurumoke Mba. You have shown that it is possible to have misunderstandings and still be so in love, you have shown me that the number of years together doesn't make love fade but helps you grow more in love whilst adjusting to being the best for each other. In all these years you have respected me in all ramifications, provided for me and most importantly showed me that you are indeed a lover of Jesus... I am happy doing this with you Adorable, you make me happy and I'd choose you every time... Let's go build a home where Jesus reigns and in turn be an example/model to the younger generation...

I love you now and always.

P.S - I couldn't have been more confident to take these next steps in life with anyone else except you My Love. You are my answered prayer... and I look forward to forever with you My Heartbeat. (Kay)
